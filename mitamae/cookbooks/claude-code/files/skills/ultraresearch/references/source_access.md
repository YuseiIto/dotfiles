# Source Access Catalog: Paywalled, Member-Only, and Japan-Specific Sources

Catalog of authoritative sources the agent cannot reach directly, plus the free
alternatives to try first. Use it to (a) route queries to the best free source,
and (b) tell the user exactly where to look when only a paywalled source will do.

**Free-first rule**: always try (or name) the free alternative before pointing
the user at a paywalled canonical source. Only recommend paid access when the
free side demonstrably lacks the needed data.

**Access tiers** (deliberately price-free; verify current pricing on the
provider's site if the user asks):

| Tier | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `free` | Openly accessible, no account needed |
| `free-with-registration` | Free after creating an account |
| `personal-subscription` | Individual paid plan or per-item purchase |
| `institutional` | University library, hospital, law firm, or corporate contract |

math), **bioRxiv/medRxiv** (life sciences), **SSRN** (social science/law),
**OSF Preprints**, **ChemRxiv**. **Unpaywall** finds legal OA copies of
paywalled papers.

## 11. Japan-Specific Sources

For research with Japanese context, check these before (or alongside) the
global databases above — English-language databases systematically miss
Japanese government statistics, case law, patents, and domestic corporate data.

### 11.1 Government Statistics and Public Data (all free)

| Resource | Authoritative for | URL |
|---|---|---|
| e-Stat (Portal Site of Official Statistics of Japan) | Cross-ministry statistics: census, economic census, vital statistics; has an API | https://www.e-stat.go.jp/ |
| RESAS (Regional Economy Society Analyzing System) | Regional economy: industry structure, population, tourism | https://resas.go.jp/ |
| MHLW statistics (厚生労働省) | Wage structure, monthly labour survey, vital statistics | https://www.mhlw.go.jp/ |
| METI statistics (経済産業省) | Industrial statistics, current survey of commerce, service industries | https://www.meti.go.jp/ |
| MIC White Paper on ICT (総務省 情報通信白書) | Annual ICT trends; pair with Statistics Bureau (https://www.stat.go.jp/) | https://www.soumu.go.jp/ |

## 12. Phrasing Template for Unreachable Sources

When a needed source is paywalled or member-only, surface it to the user with
this structure (render it in the user's language):

> I cannot access **{source}** directly, but searching it for **"{query}"**
> — e.g. by {search method: keyword, classification code, API endpoint} —
> will likely surface **{target information}**. Typical access is via
> {university library / employer contract / personal subscription}.
> For a free approximation, try **{free alternative}** with **"{alt query}"**.
> Paste back whatever you retrieve (PDF, screenshot, text) and I will
> structure, summarize, or cross-reference it.

Example: "I cannot access J-PlatPat directly, but at https://www.j-platpat.inpit.go.jp/
you can filter by patent classification and applicant (e.g. FI=G06F21/* AND
applicant=Toyota). Google Patents covers the same JP documents as a free
alternative. Export the hit list and I will aggregate it."
